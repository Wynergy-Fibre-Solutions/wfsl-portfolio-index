\# WFSL Portfolio Integrity Note



\## Purpose

This note formally asserts the integrity, scope, and immutability expectations of the WFSL portfolio as indexed in this repository.



---



\## Integrity Assertions

\- All artefacts in this repository are versioned and traceable.

\- Governance authority is delegated from wfsl-core-governance.

\- Portfolio artefacts are canonical at the time of commit.

\- Evidence files are deterministic outputs, not narratives.



---



\## Change Control

\- No artefact is modified without a version increment.

\- Superseded artefacts remain in history.

\- No silent edits. No overwrite without commit lineage.



---



\## Verification

\- Repository state reflects declared portfolio posture.

\- Trust signals are supported by evidence where applicable.

\- Investor-facing indices reference, not duplicate, source material.



---



\## Status

\- Integrity state: Locked

\- Drift tolerance: Zero
